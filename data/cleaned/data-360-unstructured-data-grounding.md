# Why Data 360 for Unstructured Data

## Summary

Data 360 powers AI solutions, including Agentforce, because it offers a robust, scalable, and secure data foundation necessary to support fast, trusted AI grounded in all your data sources.

- Unstructured Data

## Source Content

Data 360 powers AI solutions, including Agentforce, because Data 360 offers a robust, scalable, and secure data foundation. While some existing AI features don't require it, Data 360 is necessary to support fast, trusted AI grounded in all your data sources in important ways, including:

- All Types of Data: Bring in structured or unstructured data into Data 360, including audio, support chats, PDFs, and so on.
- Real-time Data: Process and react to data in seconds by using Data 360.
- Zero Copy: Expand the reach of your data by connecting data outside Salesforce by using Zero Copy Data Federation.
- Trusted Data: Feel confident in your AI experiences with our secure AI architecture, called the Trust Layer.
- Multi-Model Support: Create models or use your own model in AI Models.

This source supports the Data 360 Fundamentals domain of the Agentforce Specialist exam. It is relevant because it explains how Data 360 handles structured and unstructured data, real-time data, Zero Copy Data Federation, and multi-model support.
